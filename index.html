FAQ

Quick answers.

Is Taskist really free?

Yes. The core to-do, calendar sync, reminders, and focus timer are free forever, with no time limit. You get 50 AI requests per month on the free tier. Pro is optional, and only there if you fall in love with the AI features or premium themes.

Do my tasks leave my device?

No, not unless you sign in for cloud sync. Everything is stored locally in an OS-encrypted database. Cloud sync is strictly opt-in, and even then we only store what's needed to keep your devices in sync — no analytics, no behavioural data.

How does AI Mode work?

When you enable AI Mode, the text from your voice input is sent to Google Gemini, which extracts a list of tasks with dates, priorities, and tags. We send the minimum needed, never store the prompt on our side, and you can disable AI features in Settings at any time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed entirely through Google Play or the App Store. Cancel any time from your store account settings; you keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

What's the difference between Lifetime and Annual?

Lifetime is a one-time $119.99 purchase. You get Pro for as long as Taskist exists as a product, including all future updates we make available to Pro users. If you'll use Taskist for more than ~4 years, Lifetime wins.

Will there be an iOS version?

Yes. Android is launching first, with iOS following within a few weeks of the public launch. Your purchases will work on both — RevenueCat handles cross-platform entitlements when you sign in on the second device.
