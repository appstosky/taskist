What's inside

Everything a to-do app should be.
Nothing it shouldn't.

Six things Taskist does brilliantly — and a thousand things it deliberately doesn't. No ads, no analytics, no nonsense.

A to-do list that listens

Lists, sub-tasks, tags, priorities, and recurring rules — all the structure you actually need, with none of the friction. Snooze, postpone, or knock things out in a tap.

Your calendar, in sync

Two-way sync with your phone's native calendar. See your tasks where your meetings live, get reminded on the right device, and never miss a deadline because of a context-switch.

Reminders that respect you

Custom reminder times, exact-alarm precision, recurring schedules, and home-screen widgets — all so you only check the app when it actually has something for you.

AI that pulls its weight

Type or speak a brain-dump and AI turns it into clean separate tasks with the right dates, priorities, and lists. Auto-reschedule what's overdue. Get a one-paragraph briefing each morning.

Voice add, smart-split

Tap the mic, ramble for two minutes about your day, and AI splits it into separate tasks — handling mind-changes ("scratch that") and accents. Even works in Text-to-Speech mode without AI.

Designed to feel calm

Multiple Pro theme presets inspired by cities and landscapes. Real dark mode. Real accessibility. A focus mode that actually hides the rest of your phone. Crafted, not assembled.
