Account

Delete your Taskist account

Last updated: 18 May 2026

You can permanently delete your Taskist account and all data associated with it. This page describes how, what gets removed, and how long it takes.

Quickest option: open the app → Settings → Sign In → Delete account. This deletes your account and all cloud-synced data immediately. If you prefer to request deletion by email, use the instructions below.

Option 1 — In-app (instant)

  1. Open Taskist on your device.
  2. Go to Settings → Sign In.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your authentication record and all synced data are removed from our active systems immediately.

Option 2 — By email

If you can no longer access the app — for example, you lost the device or uninstalled before signing out — send a deletion request from the email address associated with your Taskist account to:

support@taskist.pro

Include in the email:

  • The subject line "Account deletion request"
  • The email address associated with your Taskist account

To prevent impersonation, requests must come from the same email address registered to the account. We'll confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete the deletion within 14 days at the latest.

What gets deleted

  • Your authentication record (email, hashed credential, session tokens).
  • All tasks, lists, sub-tasks, notes, tags, habits, and other content you have cloud-synced to your account.
  • Your subscription entitlement record on our side (your store account at Google Play or Apple is unaffected; manage active subscriptions there).
  • Any support correspondence linked to your account, where it can be identified.

What stays (and why)

  • Data on your device. Anything that was never synced (tasks you created without signing in) lives only on your device. Uninstall the app or clear app storage in your operating-system settings to remove it locally.
  • Encrypted backups. Our infrastructure provider keeps short-lived encrypted backups for disaster-recovery. Deleted data is purged from those backups within 90 days.
  • Records we are legally required to keep. Anonymous tax and billing records related to past subscriptions may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with applicable financial law. These records do not contain your task content.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Taskist account does not automatically cancel an active subscription on Google Play or the App Store. Cancel any active subscription separately from your store account settings before — or after — deleting your Taskist account to avoid further billing.

Questions

Email support@taskist.pro with the subject "Account deletion question" and we'll respond within a reasonable time.
